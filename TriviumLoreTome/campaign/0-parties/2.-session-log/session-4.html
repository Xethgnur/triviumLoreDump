QuickNotes
The Party has found the Hammer! It, in fact, was at the highest point in the citadel. But, they have awoken the high elven general, Vadathas, who died long ago and has been sustained by his hatred for humanity and the dragon. He interrogates the party, and Evan mistakenly admits they are here to seek the hammer for Hyseld, to which the High Elven General becomes enraged, raises his minions, and the session begins with the party entering combat. With a foe unlike any other, will the party survive this encounter!?

Following Session 3 :

Vimack Badger, Ronpip Thimble-bimble, Evan Escence, and Kashoon enter the cavern hidden within the waterfall on the cliffside near the town of Dragon's Head. At the end of the cavern, there is a room with a particular function to create tension, but is otherwise harmless, as well as an open well with bismuth liquid swirling within.

Evan dips his mace into the well. The mace now has a flaming property. He also dips his bolts in, and they receive an explosive property.

Ronpip tries to dip his sentient sword in but the sword advises against it. Ronpip instead dips his short sword in, giving it the lightning property. Ronpip dips a set of arrows in and they also receive an explosive property.

Vimack dips his short swords in, and they have poison and ice properties, respectively. He also dips 19 arrows into the vat, giving them the petrifying property.

The party enter the next room. There is a giant sign that says "Greeting Room" in large letters. There is a large statue at the centre of the room made of brass depicting a hero by the name of Havvrod, the Knight of Gold. A wind up box that Vimack begins to wind explains that this is some sort of Exhibition Room dedicated to this hero, and a new door opens. As the party explores, everything looks forlorn and left to waste. They come upon a security room with magic mirrors depicting other rooms within the facility. The party comes upon a Lost and Found area, and discover a set of paired bracelets Lost & Found and a Ion Stone of Intellect.

The party makes their way to a hall of statues, where the armours come to life! Combat Ensues against the Animated Armors.

After combating the armours, the party pushes forward, and enter an abandoned amphitheatre, a strangely clean relic room, and gallery of ancient paintings and statues. Evan renews his Detect Magic and realises that Ronpip has a magic object in his pocket. He notices its fairly powerful in its enchantment. Ronpip fumbles with it, and discovers it is an Ion Stone of Intellect. The party goes back the amphitheatre and Vimack notices a false wall, and discover a spiral stairwell going downwards that get bigger as they go down. Vimack wishes to run down the stairs in excitement and Ronpip wishes to get out of the mist by climbing up Vimack. Evan and Ronpip end up walking normally down the stairs instead.

Vimack comes upon the dragon's chamber, enormous rooms dedicated to things larger than himself. The rest of the party catches up and find Vimack gawking at the environment. Vimack decides to sneak through the area, along with his other companions. They proceed towards the observatory that appears to be under the river near Dragon's Head. At its center, a rectangular table with hologram of the town and its residents.

Caught observing the holograms, the party suddenly finds itself blown by a powerful wind and shunted into a new space unlike the dwellings of the dragon. Except for Evan, who remained, unaffected by the wind and unafraid of a golden dragon baring down on him.

Evan Escence converses with the gold dragon, revealing he and his companions went through the Exhibition Rooms, to which the Golden Dragon immediately apologises for the party having to venture through it. He brings the party back, introduces himself with many names, his true name as Hysseld but tells the party they've called him Dr. Hammond before, and asks for the rod. Vimack tries to share his wyvern to Hysseld, but Hysseld seems most disturbed by the presence of the creature. The party asks Hysseld to help protect the town in their stead, for a favor. He asks the party to retrieve his hammer from an ancient and long forgotten High Elven town where he was magically banished from. He hands a magical lute to Kashoon, which will help return the party to him. He also provides the party one magical item from his storage.

Vimack chooses the Animated Shield
Ronpip chooses a Ring of Protection
Evan chooses a The Talisman of Pure Good

Hysseld also provides a special book to Evan Escence, one that contains an extensive amount of knowledge accumulated by scribes of Fidchelle, the Aspect of Lore. Inside, Evan finds a loose piece of paper inserted into the book with a single name written on it, "Vydimal Thorrestor". Upon writing the name into the Tome of Endless Lore, he discovers The Final Written Words of Vydimal. Hysseld states he was an old friend long lost.

Evan wants more information on the town they plan to go to, and writes out Mira Esmirelda into the Tome of Endless Lore, which provides a pop-up version of the city directly from the pages of the book. Hysseld points out that his hammer would most likely be in the citadel, towards the north most end of the city.

With as much information as the party desires, Hysseld pops the party as close to the city as his magic could take them, which is a Elven gateway into the area claimed for the city. The party passes through the gates, and can feel the residual protection spell still locked in place. However, with the sun already getting low, the party decides to camp out just outside the city's gates before sun down so they can explore the forlorn town in the daylight.

Evan asks Vimack to reveal the symbol on his chest to potentially show information on it. Vimack wants to know more about the Arcane Wyvern, however, the drawing does not reveal information but asks for an entry. Evan tries to write about the Tool of War but the book also produces a page asking for an entry. The party asks about The Beak and the Leatherback Tunn and although it does not return any information on The Beak specifically, it provides a generic description of tavern. The party then asks about The Shadow Snappers, but it also comes out with some generic information, but reveals that the criminal gang has a ring spanning across Arcaedia.

Evan asks Kashoon his opinion of the party, which Kashoon answers awkwardly but honestly. Evan casts Zone of Truth and interrogates Kashoon about The Beak but he denies any knowledge about any criminals, and says he just wanted the freedom of Barddom to escape his former occupation.

Evan finally reads his House Escence's Reply that he had on him for a few days, revealing that the Noble Houses have been vying for power, and his return is requested to be expedited as being the first born son puts him in line for the throne and threatens the reputation of House Escence.

While taking watch, Ronpip Thimble-bimble takes first watch, and his anxiety keeps him on edge but nothing significant happens. Evan Escence takes second watch, and spots glowing humanoid silhouettes glowering at him and the party. Vimack Badger takes final watch, and those glowering figures are back, with more than what Evan described. He stares them down through the night...

The party picks up their camp, and enters the Emerald Mire.
