Session 01

Town Founding Caravan Travels from Massarnae

The adventure begins with the introduction of the main cast, who have been hired by Caravan Leader Yohan Sheppard in the town of Massarnae in the region of Arcaedia.

Vimack Badger, a ranger from the mountains to help guide the caravan from danger and lead the town to resources.

Ronpip Thimble-bimble, a mercenary who once fought in the Gnome Wars. Is currently a member of the Fighter's Guild Blackwood Company.

Evan Escence, a noble's son run away from home seeking the adventure he's always read about.

Along with them, Tyrian Cromwell has gathered a group of militia men to help secure the new town from dangers until proper defenses can be risen.

While the caravan rests in town and prepares to leave in the morning, the party chooses to follow Evan, who sends a courier through The Arcaedian Postal Service to his family to tell them where he is currently and where he intends to be should they send a reply, written in Evan's Letter to his Family. Vimack also wishes to send a letter, to Thimbleville.

The next morning, the party begins its 4 day journey towards the earlier scouted area that Yohan would like to start a town in. Although, during the travel, Evan is suffering from Style. Everyone is gravely concerned.

Encounter with a Nothic

Part way through the travel, at a night's rest, the party notices they are being watched by a Nothic. Tyrian ordered one of his militia to take care of it, costing a militia man as the creature retaliated.

Failure

Tyrian is displeased with the party for costing on of his men their life and has a negative outlook on them at this time.

Encounter with Bears

The party comes upon the clearing they will camp out at; Vimack would like to scout further around to make sure the area is safe. Yohan wants to start choosing the resource to focus on first. The party volunteers to scout the forest for good trees to cut down for wood, while the party suggests sending the militia to start hunting to acquire food and skins.

Success

The party has successfully convinced Tyrian to increase security by lowering the number of men to hunt.

While scouting around, the party encounters Black Bears. The party attempts to hide to avoid a fight, but are caught. Combat ensues.

Attention

Vimack has been critically injured during the fight and has a scar across his face caused by the critical attack.

The Party Disappears|The Party Goes Missing

After a short rest from the encounter, the party comes across a once sealed cave. They discover a Mysterious Cave. Inside they discover a unique symbol that almost looks like a spider on a time dial, a Time Spider. They also discover the skeletal remains of Drow elves, which seem like they have been trapped here for quite some time. Deeper still, they discover a Mirror Wall Made of Time Crystal.

Seeing their reflections, Ronpip tries to interact with his, triggering the mirror. The party is drowned in a liquid of space-time, and they are melded with a multiverse branch of themselves.

Attention

Evan drank from the crystal liquid of time. Consequences: Unknown

The Party returns to Camp

The party reawakens, finds that they have strange memories and knowledge. They are greeted by a militia from the caravan, who is as confused as they are but for different reasons. He tells the party they've been missing for three days. The militiaman tries to hurry them back to the caravan, as they have come to an emergency that requires more personal help from the party.

Upon reaching the camp, and find a medical tent has been risen at the center of camp. Inside are soldiers suffering from acid burns, bites, and scratches. They say a large black reptile attacked them while they were hunting. The party gathers information of the whereabouts of the attacker, and then proceed to rest before seeking out the attacker.

Upon waking, Vimack seeks medical attention for his ear. Ronpip needs to get questions of his chest, and Dr. Hammond recommends speaking to a man named Old Man Haggard in town. They find him in a fairly obvious spot at the center of camp. The confront him with mirror magic questions, their absence, and any assistance they may obtain. He provides them a Rod of Elements in order to assist them, but demands it back once they finish.

Before they head out to the forest, Evan checks to see if a courier has come for him but, unfortunately, one has not come. With nothing left, they head out into the forest.

Encounter with the Black Dragon Wyrmling

The party stumbles upon and takes on a Black Dragon Wyrmling. Combat ensues.

Secret

The Black Dragon Wyrmling was a spawn of an Albino Black Dragon that lives nearby, and used its mother's name in vain to threaten the party.

Success

The party successfully slays the Black Dragon Wyrmling, obtaining the venom sac that produces the caustic fluids plaguing the militia men who fought it and using its head to adorn the front gate and inspire others.

Error

The Party did not use the Rod of Elements

The party returns to camp, triumphant. But the return is ill received, as the new town's first murder has happened, and the criminal is at large.

And the Criminal Investigations begins in Session 02
