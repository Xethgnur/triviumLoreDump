Session 03

Following Session 02:

The party has taken a few weeks to gather their composure and help the town. Vimack Badger has been helping with building and major physical labor, taking advantage of his strength, as well as helping Asatir with Blacksmithing. He's been avoiding Yohan Sheppard and Tyrian Cromwell due to some conflicting inner thoughts.

Evan Escence took to the forest to meditate and recover his knowledge of his powers. He heals Vimack's hearing loss. And proceeds to use his powers to help heal town members.

Ronpip Thimble-bimble has the militia with their patrols and training. Tyrian is pleased by this development. While not helping the militia, he contributes to the engineering tasks. He has been avoiding Yohan Sheppard.

The town has since developed quite quickly, with several residential homes already up and living in. Yohan has had himself a Mayor's House built for him and his family. Tyrian now lives in a Captain's Lodge so that he no longer has to stay with his militia men. And the Cook has started a proper tavern he has dubbed The Stewmasters Flaggon.

Yohan will finally ask the three party members to see him at his home. He will be greeted and present them with a conundrum, which is naming the town. Evan suggests the name Dragon's Head and everyone agrees it is a good name. Yohan needs the party to return to Massarnae and officiate the town through Arcaedian law. Tyrian offers some horses to help the party. Asatir requests to join, for business of his own.

A couple of days of travel to Massarnae and the party arrives at the gates by morning. The guards give them some guidance. The Party is told to go to Hooke's Law District to officiate the town through legal means. Asatir seeks to go to the The Masse Market to handle his business.

The party passes through The Kepler Commons, and notice a foul odor in the air. The party acknowledges the weirdness but continues on their original mission to officiate the town. After officiating Dragon's Head as a official, legal town under Massarnae oversight, the party tries to go back to The Arcaedian Postal Service but stop at the The Kepler Commons being bothered by the smell. Ronpip wants to investigate, since it smells like dead person.

Ronpip takes the party to the market district, and seeks a perfume sales man. Upon finding one, Evan chimes in and asks for perfumes from Noble House of Escence but the man has sold out on most of them. Vimack is surprised that the man is legit about his family and history and not just a meme.

The party returns to the manhole that leads into the Sewers where the party discover two dead corpses. As the party investigates, they are attacked by Ochre Jellys.

Victory!

The party slays the Ochre Jellys

The party searches the corpses for anything useful. They find various trinkets left behind. Ronpip collects some Ooze Goo from a glass jar the party was able to pilfer from the dead bodies.

The party exits the sewers. The party asks if there is any place they can clean up after being praised by the locals. They are told of a few places. The Pinewood Cup, The Druids Grove, a place in the noble's district, a place at the slum, The Boar's Pint, and lastly the Leatherback Tunn.

The Evan decides to seek the The Arcaedian Postal Service to check on a response to his letter. He receives his reply and they decided to meet back up with Asatir in the The Masse Market.

Ronpip heads over to the Tachyon Divinity to try and feel better about his actions in the previous weeks. However, mid confession, bells start to ring and a local screams about undead in the area.

The party manages to find itself back together, with Evan and Vimack hearing all the commotion and spotting Ronpip. They spot the undead, and begin to fight them off!

Victory!

The party defeats some Zombies!

Secret

The Zombies were a result of an experiment through the High Elven locals in preparation for taking over the town come Winter

Local temple keepers provide a couple of rewards to the party for helping. Some gold, health potions, and a Glass Bottle of the Acolyte. The party heads to the The Masse Market to either find Asatir or take a moment to rest and recuperate at the Leatherback Tunn. The party notices a particular exotic shop keeper at the center of the market area, Rozahhar. Vimack decides to purchase a Strange Stone Egg from him. A spark comes from Vimack and awakens something within the egg.

Caution

Rozahhar feels swindled by this development and has taken ill will to the party. Should be come upon the party again, he will not be so welcoming to their presence again.

The party sells the gem's they've acquired and some magic scrolls they are not able to identify themselves. Vimack asks a random wizard nearby named Joe to identify them for cheap. The wizard reads them and reveals they are a Scroll of Thorn Whip and two Scroll of Chill Touch. The wizard also notices Evan's ring he received from Fidchelle the Aspect of Lore. He identifies it as the Ring of Shooting Stars. They say goodbye to Joe and pray they may meet him again.

The party manages to find Asatir again, though he has mixed feelings about having missed everything that the party went through. The Strange Stone Egg begins to crack slightly in Vimack's hand, setting Ronpip into a concern.

A bard Tiefling approaches the party to see if they were the ones that cleared the sewer and dealt with the undead. Pleasantly surprised, he asks if he could join the party on an adventure to help him write a book. He introduces himself as Kashoon. The party wants to rest for the night and refresh from their fights and soiling. Kashoon recommends the Leatherback Tunn, though he lacks any coin.

Within the Leatherback Tunn, the party are greeted by a host who introduces himself as The Pen. The party purchases five separate rooms for 40 silver, paid by Ronpip Thimble-bimble. Ronpip gives Kashoon 5 gold pieces and tells him to get a drink. Vimack takes Kashoon to the bar front and speak to The Rain for drinks. Vimack then gives him an additional 29 gold pieces, leaving Kashoon with 39 gold pieces on his person. Evan and Vimack head upstairs to bathe and rest. Evan notices that all of his shampoos are of House Escence products, but takes no further thought into it. And the party falls asleep into their bathes...

Only to find themselves moved from their rooms into a basement area greeting room, still naked and still in their bathes. A Kenku is at a desk taking notes and repeats what the party is saying instinctually. Vimack tries to approach the Kenku, but a Tiefling steps out to stop him. Behind the Tiefling, The Beak steps out from the shadows to stand by the Kenku. The Beak demands the party not interfering in The Shadow Snappers activities, revealing they are some sort of criminal ring with ties to Dragon's Head. The party tries to justify they will not interfere if The Beak does better, though The Beak simply finds it amusing. The party is allowed to leave, and they come back to Kashoon, and find Asatir is very drunk.

The party decides to remain in Massarnae to do some additional research on the tower deed and a few other things. They head to the library per recommendation of the town guard. Vimack tries to find more information on the Strange Stone Egg that Vimack has, but information seems fairly scarce. Evan is interested in the Illvidri Heralds, but ends up showing him entries on Storm Gods to catch Vimack's interest. Vimack is more interested in the egg however. Ronpip seeks a librarian, and they try to present the egg to the librarian for more info. However, the egg begins to open.

Out comes out a baby wyvern of strange anatomy. Evan loses his shit and Vimack activates his motherly instincts. Ronpip is concerned. Ronpip and Evan cannot find info in the library for what it eats, so Ronpip presents it rations; this aggravates the wyvern hatchling and it fires an Arcane Missile from its mouth at Ronpip. Ronpip is unphased.

The party decides to head back to Dragon's Head with Kashoon in tow. Evan asks if the wyvern has a name, but Vimack has not named it though he has an idea. Nearly there to Dragon's Head, the party hears a Dragon's Roar emanating from the forest. Two strangers run towards and away from the party. A Hysseld crashes through the forest chasing after them, and tells the two in common to not return. The party greets it, and startled, it runs back into the forest. Vimack chases after it, but the dragon polymorphs into a deer to outrun him. The party loses the deer at a log that crosses the river as a natural bridge, with the deer's tracks implying that it has jumped into the river.

The party realizes there is a waterfall at the cliff near the town. The party decides they want to give the deed and then investigate the cliff. The party gives Yohan Sheppard the town deed. Yohan Sheppard rewards the party with 100 gold each for their troubles.

The party decides since the day is still new, to investigate the cliff waterfall across the river. They shimmy through a narrow ridge behind the waterfall, where a cavern has been secreted within where something has been residing. Exploring the complex in Session 04
