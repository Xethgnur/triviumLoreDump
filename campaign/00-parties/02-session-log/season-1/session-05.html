Session 05

Following Session 04 :

Ronpip Thimble-bimble , Evan Escence , Vimack Badger , and Kashoon pack up their camp from just outside Mira Esmirelda and enter the town. They start by heading towards the plaza passed the rotted buildings that are slowly being consumed by the swamp. Seeing nothing left about to find, they head to the temple area. At the center, they find a statue of Angharradh made of moonstone, untouched by the swamps corruptions. Ronpip regards how much the statue might be worth, and its apparent that it would be worth a lot of coin. This makes him wonder why the people would just leave such an expensive statue.

With nothing else catching their interest, they move to the citadel. Vimack Badger tears open the gates to allow the party to enter into the area. The party enters the citadel, and it is dark, dank, and stale air. Exploring through, they first encounter a Wraith and Specters of long dead elven soldiers who demand them to leave.

Fail

Ronpip shoots Vimack in the ass with a Volcanic Arrow, setting it on fire

Success

The party successfully defeats the incorporeal enemies.

After the encounter, Vimack continues to explore, and stumbles into a room with a Banshee crying inside. Vimack panics and tries to shut the door, but Ronpip opens it, taking a swipe. She wails and shakes the walls, knocking Kashoon and Ronpip out. Evan and Vimack manage to bring Kashoon and Ronpip back to consciousness, and the party defeats the Banshee.

Success

The party successfully defeats the Banshee

The party takes a Short Rest, and then continue to search for the Hammer. They come upon a tapping noise, and investigate, only to be spotted by and attacked by a Revenant, that specifically targets Evan.

Fail

The Revenant critically strikes Evan, and damages him to the point of near death.

Success

Vimack successfully slays the Revenant

Evan discovers a black ring in the ashes of the Revenant and pockets it.

The party then decides they need to take a long rest before continuing to explore, braving the potential dangers by setting up camp within the citadel. The dangers testing their resolve in Session 06
