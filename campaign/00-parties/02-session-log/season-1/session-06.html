Session 06

Following Session 05 :

Vimack Badger , Ronpip Thimble-bimble , Kashoon , and Evan Escence pickup their camp after resting for an extended amount of time to heal and recover, and unbarricade the doors to continue exploring the citadel for the hammer in Mira Esmirelda.

While exploring, the party comes across a tapestry depicting the goddess Angharradh blessing the Great General Vadathaus Horreneth, a blessed leader who conquered swathes of human territory in the long ended war between ancient Arcaedians and the High Elves.

Note

Kashoon have given Evan Escence his dagger to help him strike with melee attacks.

The party continues to explore, and trigger a trap.

Fail

Vimack takes significant damage from a falling stone block.

At the end of the hallway where the trap was triggered, a chest lays in wait. The chest is trapped and releases a puff of gas. Inside is 800 copper, 4000 silver, 1800 gold, and 138 platinum coins, a leather coin purse, a copper clothed tabard, an ornate silver mirror, an ornate ivory drinking horn, lapis urn, platinum amulet, and 3 spell scrolls, and two potions. One is red, and one is brownish.

They explore further and discover a rather well preserved anvil, however, the party are unable to find a way to take it with them. Vimack crashes through the next door, where the west side of the room contains a kiln and coal hold. In the opposite corner of the room, a large round mass cries.

The party tries to sneak around the Beholder Zombie but Kashoon trips and creates noise.

Fail

Vimack critically misses the beholder and the fear effect of the Beholder Zombie nearly gets to him.

Success

Kashoon kills the Beholder Zombie

Evan scribes the Beholder Zombie into the Tome of Endless Lore and comes to find that the beholder is a little out of place for the area. Evan investigates a pile of rubble and discovers another black ring.

Evan then thinks he should burn the Beholder Zombie corpse they had recently killed.

Fail

Evan poisons himself from the smoke caused by burning the zombie flesh of the Beholder Zombie

Ronpip pushes forward but steps on a magical sigil that causes him to be paralyzed. The party pulls him out easily but he remains paralyzed. Vimack slaps him in the face to slap him to try and break him out of the paralysis, though it does nothing. Ronpip feels everything. The party creates a plan to try and cross the magic sigil, and then Ronpip is freed from his plight, face hurting. He pulls out rope, and jumps the gap.

Fail

Ronpip tries to jump the gap, but everything that could have gone wrong, went wrong. However, Ronpip is able to overcome the paralysis.

One by one, they jump the corner and avoid the trap's antics any further.

Further up the hall, the party pushes further, and come across a Ghast and three Ghouls gnawing at bones and rotted flesh. Ronpip initiates with Create Bonfire to surprise them.

Success

The party eliminates the Ghast and Ghouls

The party goes up stairs and come across two large iron doors at the top. Vimack suggests resting before entering the iron doors. The party tries to rest, and the citadel taunts them in their rest, testing their resolve. But each of them manages to ignore the disquiet around them. They rest until 3am, pack up, and enter the room beyond the iron doors.

Inside is a long destroyed by time War Room, where Vadathaus Horreneth sits at the end of the table, and his generals still sit at the sides. Behind Vadathaus Horreneth lies a silver and gold warhammer, the one they sought at the behest of Hysseld. As the party enters the room Vadathaus Horreneth begins to stir, Ronpip casts Shatter. Vadathaus Horreneth retaliates with a Fear attack.

Evan tries to have a civil discussion with Vadathaus Horreneth, who is already displeased by his specific presence. However, Evan mentions they were sent by Hysseld to retrieve the hammer, which triggers his anger, displeased that the party is in league with a dragon, and that the elves will rule again when they obtain the Facets of Virtue.
