Session 08

Following Session 07 :

Hysseld drops off Evan Escence, Ronpip Thimble-bimble, Vimack Badger, and Dorizoul just north of the forest where he encourages the party to speak with the Summerset Princess. He drops them off at a set of hidden gates, made of branches and plant life. Hysseld warns the party to remain on the path, no matter what they hear or see. Vimack Badger takes the lead, and takes the party deep into the forest as far as he could tell. The stone slab path weaves between the trees. Evan Escence detects they Fey are nearby through his Cursed Ring of Seething, and takes caution. Vimack begins to hear the sound of something struggling off the edge of the path. Being a being of goodness within his large heart, Vimack feels compelled to help what ever creature this is. A couple feet off the path, Vimack comes across a Tiddlefiddle McBottomfondler stuck. Meanwhile, Evan and Ronpip hold on a rope tied to Vimack to make sure he can return the path with no issues, or pull him if he is danger. Vimack carefully frees the Tiddlefiddle McBottomfondler. The Tiddlefiddle McBottomfondler thanks him and introduces himself as Tiddlefiddle McBottomfondler, to which he then asks Vimack "May I have your name?". Vimack shakes the Tiddlefiddle McBottomfondler's hand, and gives the Tiddlefiddle McBottomfondler his name, which causes a puff of pink smoke to blast between them, as now their names are forever(?) exchanged.

Evan Escence and Ronpip Thimble-bimble proceed to question Tiddlefiddle McBottomfondler about what just happened. This only angers Tiddlefiddle McBottomfondler. In an attempt to chase after the Tiddlefiddle McBottomfondler that stole his name, Tiddlefiddle McBottomfondler gets his in the back of his head from a log trap and is dropped into a bright blue portal. Ronpip and Evan do their best to contain the rope, but the force of the rope drops Evan to his side as he is dragged into the forest. He tries to grow claws through My Immortal, but the earth is soft the roots don't hold. Ronpip tries to anchor himself down with his weapon, but his grip on the rope and his weapon do not save him. The three of them are left freefalling through the portal, and are thrown out on the otherside, dropping into The Feywild, a plane of dreams, whimsy, and the fey. Before the party could consider recovering, the portal closes behind them and forms a gnarled wooden hole within a tree.

The party goes through several strange events that test their patience, wits, and willingness to deal with whimsical obstructions. This includes a path that loops no matter the direction taken, a satyr who really wants to share his drink, a lake that plays an enticing tune to draw strangers into it, a litter of blink dog pups and their mom, sticky mud trap set up by some tricksters, a brook with a tree man named Kenobade sitting on a boulder while sheparding other tree kin who provides them directions to someone who can help them leave the Feywild, a big rock with a tiny village next to it, little bramble people riding bramble lizards, and a massive lake containing many crabs spawned from a building sized crab.

Finally passed all these obstacles, the party follows a river upstream and comes upon a mushroom house with a watermill. A gnome greets them. He's confused as to why people of the Material Plane are doing in the Feywild. Ronpip explains they were tricked by a Tiddlefiddle McBottomfondler into falling into a portal, and TIddlefiddle McBottomfondler says his name was taken. Understanding, the gnome friend prepares a portal back into the Material Plane.

The party finds themselves back in the Summer Forest, but near the city of wood gnomes called Cumberbatch. The gnome friend guides the party through the city, and has to hand Vimack and Evan a special silk mask for them to breath freely from the magical enchantment that would normally put strangers to sleep. He introduces himself as Bonley Shroomcap. He leads them to the Elder's Tree where Elder Torgey Snailback agrees to help the party, specifically Tiddlefiddle McBottomfondler. The Elder prepares a ritual, and returns Vimack Badger's name to him. Relieved, they ask to be guided to the Wood Elven capital to speak with the Summerset Princess. Elder Torgey Snailback volunteers Bonley Shroomcap to take them there for skipping out on his duty days.

He leads the party to Wood Elven city of Bryturdel. The townsfolk do not pay them much mind, and are oddly helpful to them. They direct them to the palace at the edge of town where they can speak to the Summerset Princess about their mission. The party is continued to be surprised at how unguarded the entrance, the town is, and the palace gates. Even the guards are oddly nice to them. When asked if they can see the Summerset Princess, one of the guards has to go check something. He momentarily comes back with General Orthorion who questions them. Vimack reveals that they have come about the High Elves potentially invading. He is skeptical, but allows them passage into the palace. As they walk, the party talks and reveals more details to General Orthorion. He takes them into a Conservatory that acts as the throne room, where the Summerset Princess sits within. She leaves her space and approaches the party with an aura of authority and power. She asks what the party desires with her presence. They are as honest with her as they were with General Orthorion. The Summerset Princess waves the guards of the room off, and her personality switches, revealing her true fey nature. She asks if the party has any proof, but the party has none. Orthorion insists that he and the party can check on The Tree-ty on an island just outside the summer forest. The Summerset Princess accepts, and allows the party to rest and to meet with Orthorion in the morning.

The party meets with Orthorion and a small squadron of elven guard. They take to the water with specially made canoes that wade through the river and into the ocean, and on to The Tree-ty Island. Orthorion and his guards lead the party upon an old path, where at the center of this small island and an intertwined tree of two different species. At first, it appears as though both are standing, but upon closer inspection, one of the two trees has begun to fade and die within. Orthorion, in disbelief, tests it with touch and is tense.

Without warning, the party and the guards are ambushed. The two elven guard brought along were immediately shot through the necks, and the party draws their weapons and prepares themselves for combat in Session 09!
