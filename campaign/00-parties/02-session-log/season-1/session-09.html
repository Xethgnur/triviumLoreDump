Session 09

Following Session 08

Ronpip Thimble-bimble , Evan Escence , and Vimack Badger , along side General Orthorion, find themselves ambushed on The Tree-ty Island by unknown assailants, who have dropped two of their escorts. Evan sprouts wings, creates a wall of force to try and separate leaders from lackeys; and moves forward to get an advantage, which draws fire to him.

Critical Damage!

Evan is Critically Wounded by one of the Assassins with an arrow and drops the psionic wall

A Druid summons a large bol of moths that bite into Evan while he is in the air, obscuring Evan's vision and Evan from the party. Vimack and Ronpip try to concentrate on one assassin, but he's dodging everything they are throwing at him.

Evan flies straight up to escape the cloud of moths, and uses his Ring of Shooting Stars to fire a ball of lightning at a priestess and a cloaked figure, but they side step the spell. Two assassins attack Vimack, one drawing two arrows that his Animated Shield blocks, but the one in Melee manages to draw his long sword and gets two swipes in. Another shoots at Dorizoul. Another fires two at Evan Escence but misses. The Priests fires a sickly beam of green energy at Evan Escence.

There appears to be an additional enemy that is not making any move or taking any action. He seems to only be observing the party.

Ronpip uses his Tool of War to aim his fireball at a central location, singing several attackers. General Orthorion manipulates his arrows to curve around cover.

Critical Strike!

Vimack recklessly attacks and manages to finally score a hit on the assassin who has been dodging him, and it is a critical hit!

With inspiration of the critical hit, he also manages to get his second sword into the assassin. He whistles for Dorizoul to move into position, and she comes in running for a Trample, and knocks him prone. Since Vimack is raging, lightning arcs from his body into the target but he manages to just narrowly dodge a direct hit.

Evan Escence channels psionic energy to direct Ronpip to a location psychically, and then he flies into cover to cure his wounds from the heavy attacks he sustained.

The assassins draw their arrows, two hitting Ronpip and two hitting Vimack and one hitting Dorizoul. The Druid tosses an ice knife at her prone ally, which does not hit but still explodes into ice shards.

Ronpip turns his Tool of War into a sword and goes into melee, missing his first strike but hitting his second. Othorion continues to pelt his intended target. Vimack strikes with two attacks and kills the assassin by taking his short swords straight through his chest. Dorizoul charges to her shooter and Tramples him over. Vimack whistles and she kicks him while he is prone, but his armor protects him from her stomps. Vimack moves over in preparation, and lightning arcs from his body to the now prone attacker. Evan channels his psionic powers and creates an area of darkness, covering the area of the druid, the priestess, and the being who has only been watching the entire combat. He then calls upon My Immortal to attack the assassin with Ronpip, but she misses as he parries her.

The assassin with Ronpip manages to catch one hit in with his frosted longsword against Ronpip. One tries to make hail mary shots towards Evans but miss terribly. The prone assassin attacks from prone, with one strike hitting Vimack at the legs with his own frosted longsword. The priestess in darkness attempts to dispel the darkness but finds she could not.

Ronpip channels a Second Wind and attacks his target while casting Booming Blade across his sword. Sonic energy surrounds the assassin.

Critical Strike!

Ronpip makes a second strike critically striking, and goes from the right side of his neck and through his armor to his arm, bisecting him.

Othorion continues to curve his arrows around cover. Vimack continues his rage and strikes out against the prone assassin, but only one short sword hits. Dorizoul follows up with kicks, which Vimack follows up with another short sword attack, and then bears down his lightning strike. Evan commands My Immortal to strike and she fills the assassin with holes with blinding speed of her rapier. Evan then compels Vimack to attack twice again, in which one of the strikes find purchase.

The assassin that was prone will flip back to his feet. Vimack manages to get a hit on him, but the assassin retaliates with a strike of his own, dropping Vimack. The druid transforms within the sphere darkness.

Ronpip tosses his sword in Othorion's direction, and then teleports to it, telling him "We need to get outta here!" but Othorion does not seem to take heed. He instead fires two arrows into the darkness and knows he hit, before moving to cover. Dorizoul does her best defend Vimack, and she blasts him with her arcane breathe attack. Evan calls upon Healing Word to revive Vimack.

Meanwhile, nothing has happened within the sphere of darkness. No one has entered or left.

Ronpip rallies Vimack and gives him an extra moment of vigor. Othorion continues to fire upon something in the sphere of darkness with his bow and arrows, using magical enchantments that the party does not understand. The party can hear the sounds of a canine getting wounded but see nothing. Vimack stands and consumes a potion to help heal his wounds from battle. Everyone repositions themselves until Evan can drop the sphere of darkness. Evan compels Othorion to make additional attacks, and then dispels the darkness. Inside, a white blue wolf stands at the ready to attack.

The wolf moves forward and then howls an icey gale at Ronpip, Vimack, and Dorizoul. Dorizoul manages to protect her vitals, but Ronpip and Vimack take the full icey bite of the attack.

Ronpip backs up and conjures a barrage of spectral daggers at the wolf with one of the daggers he obtained from Asatir. Othorion drops his bow and goes into melee with the wolf, striking twice causing icey blood to freeze upon the ground nearby. Vimack knocks and arrow and fires a brambled arrow to the corner of the island, causing a bramble patch to bloom rapidly, reaching the wolf. He then concentrates on the wolf and marks it with Hunter's Mark. Evan speaks words of healing with Mass Healing Word to provide more health to the party, and allows Othorion to make additional attacks.

The wolf lunges at Othorion, gnashes him in her jaws as she shakes him with gore shakes, dropping him prone and bleeding. The priestess creates a portal near her and the cloaked figure, one of inky blackness that seems to tear a hole into the space.

Ronpip gathers eldritch energy in his blade, and fires it at the wolf, the damage causing the wolf to revert back into her humanoid form. She is looking like she is on her last legs. Ronpip then takes cover. Othorion crawls away while she is momentarily distracted. Vimack aims with his longbow, and fires one of the petrifying arrow, hitting her in the back. Slowly it turns her to stone, and she is petrified into a statue.

The cloaked figure and priestess step through the portal. And hostilities end.

Ronpip wants to get Othorion back to the Summerset Princess, but Evan and Vimack try to pillage the remains. Ronpip, now interested, cuts the head off of one of the dead assassins and takes a mask. They acquire some additional arrows for their quivers. Evan gives Othorion a Healing Potion in order to help with the gash in his side. Vimack tries to pick up a longbow, but it crumbles in his hand like weak ice. They pick up the wood elven guard and pack the bodies into the canoes. They drag the remaining bodies of the high elf assassins to the cliffside and drop them into the ocean for the fish to consume. They rip the staff out of the petrified druid's hand, breaking it in half(and thus causing it to lose its magic), before dumping her statued body into the ocean as well.

The party returns to the canoes to join Othorion, and they paddle back to Bryturdel. Othorion calls for guards to care for the bodies, and then takes the party back to the Summerset Princess to discuss the events and what to do further. Othorion vouches for the party's words and stands by them for their cause.

The party recalls that there is no emperor on the seat of Arcaedia, and the nobles are still fighting over it. Evan says he can convince his family to help, but he cannot vouch for the other noble houses, and one of his friends from the Noble House of Zepplin, Ledd has been hospitalized during this vie for power. He says he has good relations with the Noble House of Mumford and the Noble House of Bellion but does not have good diplomacy with the Noble House of Paramoure or the Noble House of Maroon. They come to an agreement for the Summerset Princess to have her send her militia to the Capital City, as long as the party can put someone in power to accept the militia as part of a defense against an invasion. She offers to allow them guided passage through the Fey Realm to the Capital, or offer them High Bred Forest Stags to get them to Thimbleville. The party decides to go to the Capital.

The party returns to an inn, drinking mead to relax after battle and exchanging stories to continue to build comradery between them. The party goes to bed, and Evan is pulled into a dreamscape, and finds himself with a strange blue humanoid creature who says his name is Nebun. He considers himself some sort of leader of the Scribes of Fidchelle, and demands Evan to stop meddling in affairs and perform as they were meant to. Observe and write. Nothing more. Evan, however, feels a conflict of interest.

The party awakens. Evan mentions he had a strange dream. Vimack mentions he dreamed of "his angel". And the party proceeds back to the Palace to utilize a fey portal and guide to get to The Capital quickly. She creates a portal within a nearby tree, and from within a beetle like creature will come out. She introduces it as Bumblebop who will guide them to the intended direction and destination. This portal feels different from their last time they travelled through.

Bumblebop guides them through, scaring away any fey that might bother them and makign sure the party keeps up with him. They come across a tree ent, that Bumblebop stops for, and the party discovers its Kenobade. He greets the party and then bids them good luck on their journey. They also come across another adventuring party along the way. They come upon a Hobo, A Pouty Half Elf, and a Lizardman who thinks he is a Dragonborn They exchange some words of encouragement and then move on. They come across the destination through the portal, and pass through. They did not arrive too far from the Capital but it will still take a bit of a walk. Bumblebop gives the party a parting salute, and goes back to the Fey Realm, where the portal to becomes a gnarled tree once more.

Evan sighs in relief of finally returning to urban civil society. Evan takes the party to the Southern Gate, pointing out places that he's visited just outside the walls with excitement. Once passed the gates, Evan wishes the party to come to his noble estate. Vimack is perturbed by the urban shaped trees and cut weeds the nobles use for their low cut fields. Evan leads the party to the gates of House Essence, and is greeted by his favourite butler Paul McCoy. Paul opens the gates for the party and leads them to the Mansion. Evan asks about the famaily; Paul says that his brother is away but everyone else is in house, including Evan's master Ed Sher'ran. The party must relieve themselves of their weapons at the entrance of the mansion. Evan decides to meet with his father, first and foremost. They enter his office, and he immediately offers his son a deep bear hug embrace. Evan introduces the party to his father, and he introduces himself as George Escence. After Evan describes his adventure, he also shares the dire need to set someone in power and asks about Ledd. His father shares as much as he knows, and calls upon a family meeting to have a proper discussion. Evan's mother joins first, then his sister who is very upset that he did not see her first, and while Evan tries to play up his return, his mother keeps things in scope, and respectfully recognize Evan is taking things a little more seriously since his departure a year ago. The family discusses current events among the noble houses. George reminds Evan is he becomes Emperor that his adventures end, and Evan states maybe he could still use his presence to check things out and bring out potential dark ends. George then goes on about the noble houses:

  • The Noble House of Escence has made a very substantial and successful business from alchemical practices related to person hygiene and cleaning rumored to have harsh labor practices
  • The Noble House of Van Halen have made their name through their diplomacy and social skills, though rumor has it they made a deal with a devil to obtain such diplomatic status.
  • The Noble House of Foo has made itself known as one of the best private security organizations with some of the best trained guardsmen gifted with Wuzhia lineage, but there is question of their loyalty since they are not entirely Arcaedian native.
  • The Noble House of Paramoure has control over the religious temples and districts; funding, caring, and employing them with various members. Though, such deep religious ties makes them an easy target for cult activity
  • The Noble House of Maroon were once famous explorers and are one of the oldest noble houses, tying back to the founding days of The Arcaedian Dominion with their cartography and scribes for Arcaedian history. Though, they have the lowest relationships with other houses.
  • The Noble House of Chevelle have a deep lineage of arcane practices and control the arcane schools of wizardry and libraries of arcane knowledge; though it is clear their lineage is taking a toll on them and people fear a curse has befell them.
  • The Noble House of Bellion has a monopoly on fine wines and culinary feasts, which makes them a target for black market ingredients, potentially even Canabalization.
  • The Noble House of Urie are information brokers, as well as one of the largest and richest houses in part of their well developed social status. They seek the throne most of all, especially Brand Diodah Urie
  • The Noble House of Mumford are a house of bards and have some of the best performances and performers across the land, some event hink they are blessed by some sort of god-like being.

He mentions that the net meeting of the noble houses is in two days, where Evan and party can see the chaos for themselves. With nothing more to discuss, Evan decides to seek out Ed Sher'ran, and George extends his house to the party as their own.

In the Grand Garden of House Escence, Ed Sher'ran is meditating in the garden. Evan summons My Immortal and they join in on the meditation. They exchange some fitting words, Ed believing Evan has grown stronger since he has last seen him. Evan introduces his friends. Evan believes his friends are better than he, but Ed Sher'ran disagrees, and wishes Evan to show him.

Evan proudly accepts the challenge. He and My Immortal pose up in battle preparation. Ed Sher'ran twists into a pose requiring incredible flexibility and summons EarthWindenFire, his own battle spirit. And the two prepare for battle in Session 10!
