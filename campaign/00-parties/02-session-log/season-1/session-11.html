Session 11

Following Session 10 :

Evan Escence takes on the Tullum Pugna Nula requested by Brand Diodah Urie, taking him and his companions, Vimack Badger, Ronpip Thimble-bimble, Lorellai, and Paul McCoy to the amphitheater that acts as their arena to face off against Brand and his own allies. Tylos initiates the combat to begin, and the two parties attack.

Ronpip immediately summons the Tool of War and casts Shatter upon the closest member of Brand's group. A red dragonborn rips his cloak off and charges towards Ronpip, but Ronpip is too quick with his Greatsword. With intensity, he attacks Ronpip again, and manages to get a strike in, though Ronpip parries the attack to lessen its blow. Next, a cloaked Half-Elf dashes in and strikes at Ronpip, summoning green flames across his blade.

DOWNED

Ronpip is incapacitated from the strike!

Vimack spawns an area of thorns and spikes upon the ground before Brand's group, making it difficult for them to move. A human with a mace and shield climbs through the Spike Growth and waves a hand, sending a wave of necrotic energy at Vimack. Paul stays where he is and prepares. Brand and King of the Clouds send a wave of psychic energy across Evan's party, causing Vimack to nearly hit Dorizoul and also causes him to feel frightened of Brand and his menacing presence. The Tiefling Wizard casts a red hot beam of energy at Paul. Evan casts Mystic Missile at several targets, infusing his arcane missiles with his psionic influence. The Cleric is hit and is Charmed, seeing Evan as an ally. The Figher is hit and is Dazed. The Rogue manages to overcome the effects but is still damaged by the spell. Lorellai begins a Prayer of Healing and heals her allies from the initial hits. Ronpip is revived, and tosses his blade at the rogue who promptly dodges it. He then moves out of the way, with a plan in mind. The Red Dragonborn has to shake his head and recover from the Dazed blow. Vimack compels himself, brings himself to a rage, and fires from his longbow at Brand, missing one shot but hitting with his second shot. He then whistles and Dorizoul charges arcane energy into her mouth and releases it, hitting Brand, the Cleric, and the Red Dragonborn with a flurry of arcane missiles. The Cleric attempts to move forward to attack the Arcane Wyvern, but ends up stabbing himself and is too weak to get through the floor of bramble and thorns. Paul gets confirmation from Evan to proceed, and then proceeds to attack the red dragonborn with a surprising amount of speed, only hitting once but hitting with enough force to Stun him. Brand points at Paul and puts him in a state of shock with the illusion of Evan dying. The Tiefling Wizard makes a sign of the shadows, and summons a Hound of Ill Omen from his own shadow to target Evan, which then sprints through the brambles and thorns to get to him. He attempts to then summon the weave to cast Fireball but Evan uses Counterspell to rip the arcane energy away from the Wizard. Evan takes up The Talisman of Pure Good and attempts to send Brand straight to hell, but Brand absconds from it and is unaffected. Lorellai rushes in, and attacks the red dragonborn, smiting with her flail in hand twice, and then shield bashes into him into the brambles and thorns, knocking him out of combat. The Hound of Ill Omen charges Evan, and locks its jaw into his arm, pulling him to the ground Prone. Ronpip Pulls out his shield while tossing a dagger, using it to Conjure Barrage, striking Brand and his lackeys with spectral daggers. He then hops on to Dorizoul and summons his sword to his hands, willing it into a lance as it reforms. The rogue dives through the brambles and attacks Lorellai quickly to then disengage into a better position for himself. Vimack drops his bow and pulls out his second sword to attack the Hound of Ill Omen that is attacking Evan.

CRITICAL HIT!

Vimack manages to strike the Hound of Ill Omen with a reckless critical strike and strikes it with lightning!

Paul runs to Evan's aid, and hits the rogue with a flurry of unarmed strikes, hitting the Swashbuckler three times and knocking them out of the fight! Brand creates a whirlwind around him, and flies over the brambles. He channels psionic energies, and blasts an intense gust of wind to knock Ronpip and Dorizoul away, but it does nothing to the wyvern or gnome. The Tiefling Wizard bites his thumb and creates a demonic circle upon the ground in blood. It opens up, and a fiendish hand reaches up from the abyss and climbs out. Ronpip recognizes that its not an arcane spell. Lorellai recognizes it as a Summon Lesser Demon spell. Vimack sees in the crowd a particular noble performing subtle arcane gestures. Evan from the ground casts Mystic Missile. The Hound of Ill Omen is struck and receives a sudden wave of Exhaustion. The demon and the wizard are hit, with the wizard falling to the ground from the hit. Evan then has Caesar move towards Brand but the stench of the fiend causes it to feel suddenly sick. Still, it reaches over to Brand and Evan channels Shocking Grasp through it. He tries to get up to his feet, but the Hound of Ill Omen keeps Evan to the ground. Lorellai rushes at Hezrou, passing through the waft of the demon's stench. She strikes at it with her flail, though her strikes seem to do little damage, her holy smites seem to hit for a lot. She then bashes it with her shield. The Hound of Ill Omen mauls Evan while he remains locked to the ground. The summoned fiend attacks wide, swiping for Lorellai, Dorizoul, and Ronpip, though Dorizoul and Lorellai manage to get out of the way. Ronpip draws upon the magic of the Tool of War and gives himself temporary vigor while also stilling his warrior soul for real vigor. Then, with intensity in a surge of energy, he attacks Brand with his lance and channels Booming Blade through it but Brand dramatically dodges out of the way of the attacks. Vimack dives in at the Hound of Ill Omen recklessly.

CRITICAL STRIKES!

Both swords come in and cross section into the Hound of Ill Omen, lifting it off the ground and forcing it to dissipate in shadowy smoke.

Vimack then moves to the edge of the arena, and tries to call out the noble who was making the arcane gestures he was noticing. But the noble does not expose themselves, and Tylos is getting concerned of Vimack's actions. Vimack tries to explain his actions to Tylos, but Tylos is not convinced and is prepping to be defensive, turning Vimack back to the battle and implying consequences to the alternative. Paul helps Evan stand up from being mauled by the Hound of Ill Omen. Brand is mad that the Flumph shocked him, and flicks Caesar with psychic energy, obliterating it.

Fail

Caesar was killed in battle.

Brand then uses his Psionic Energy to cloak himself in a spinning funnel of air. Lorellai tries to maneuver away from the fiend, but it sees an opportunity to bite into her. Evan draws upon psionic energies and casts a beam of Radiant energies at the summoned demon, blinding it and forcing it to let her go. Lorellai dives in to hit Brand, misses and he manipulates the flail to hit her back, but it misses her. She shifts her weight and hits the summoned demon instead, smiting it across the side. The summoned demon, enraged by the blinding effect, tries to maneuver towards Evan, or where it felt it was being shot at. Ronpip and Lorellai take advantage of its lapse in judgement and strike! Lorellai manages to get a strike and smite in.

CRITICAL STRIKE!

Ronpip manages to strike in with his lance into a vital area.

The summoned fiend lashes out to the general area of Evan, but misses with its wild strikes. Ronpip goes after Brand again with Booming Blade channeling upon the lance. Brand has to swiftly dodge as Ronpip is starting to read his movements, and the second jab catches him, striking into his shoulder and enveloping him with sonic energy. Tylos and Evan tell Vimack to get back into the fight. Vimack is still angry about the noble interfering and climbs off the stage. Tylos draws his sword and takes an attack against Vimack, but Vimack swipes it away with a short sword and proceeds towards the noble. He tries to reason angrily with the noble to dispel the summoning, but the noble ignores him to the best of their ability. He returns to the stage upset, and gets close to the summoned fiend, striking it with the collected static charge within his body. Brand lashes into Ronpip's mind with a psionic whip. Evan moves away from the summoned demon, and it tries to take a swipe at the movement. My Immortal comes in and shields Evan from the bite. Evan draws upon his psionic energy and tries to shove Brand. He stands his ground and is unmoved but is still pummeled by the force of the blow. Lorellai moves away from Brand, and Brand takes a swipe at her with his axe, gashing into her armor and his gauntlets channel psychic energy into her, knocking her out of the fight. The summoned fiend goes for Vimack as he is the biggest and closest target. In its wild strikes, its claws rake across Vimack but he gets out of the way of its bite. Ronpip continues to pierce at Brand, adjusting into a Precision Strike, hitting him and once again dousing him in sonic energy.

Ronpip Kills Brand

Ronpip strikes him directly into the eye, channeling his sonic energy into his head, and causes his head to explode.

Before he died, Brand stumble about and states He his future was to become the Emperor, "Madame Darlayia predicted it! She promised me a crowd, the wine would run through the streets" as his entire head explodes.

The demon finds itself unsummoned, trying to claw its self back to the material plane but gets sucked away.

Tylos then declares that Evan of the Noble House of Escence has won the battle. Ronpip is sickened by the cheers and the aftermath. Vimack is in a battle rush, cheering with Evan by picking him up, before they realize that Lorellai is bleeding out on the ground. Ronpip rides Dorizoul to Lorellai, and with a Healer's Kit stabilizes her. Vimack angrily picks her up. Evan plays up the victory with some effects, before realizing that his friends are extremely upset and wish to leave. Tylos congratulates Evan for his victory, and reveals that the Noble House of Escence now takes all from the Noble House of Urie, merging in favor to the Noble House of Escence. Evan is stunned and pleased, though Ronpip reminds him that its less people now to help the country. Evan asks about a healer for Lorellai and Tylos gives healing through his hand into Lorellai. She awakens with a startle but Vimack, although angry, keeps her calm.

The party looks into the remains of Brand's companions. Brand's has some unique Bracers that intrigue Evan. The Tiefling Wizard has a Wand of Fireballs. The red dragonborn is wearing Smoldering Plate Armor and dons Burning Gauntlets. The Rogue is wearing Speedy Boots. The Cleric has a Necklace of Prayer Beads.

Evan wishes to go see what he has inherited. Ronpip stares at the dead bodies and contemplates his lance. Paul accompanies Vimack and Lorellai back to the Escence Estate. Ronpip joins them after having a momentary discussion with the Tool of War. Evan gives Vimack the spoils to Identify later. Vimack is still upset about the summoned demon and huffs off when it seems Evan does not know what happened.

Evan heads to the Financial District to discuss the acquired assets. Evan explains the situation to an associated, who takes him to a private room while they collect the paperwork pertaining to the Noble House of Urie.

Meanwhile, Ronpip and Vimack discuss what he saw; Lorellai is kicking in his arms. Vimack states he saw one of the nobles in the audience summoned the demon and continues to be angry. Until he starts to hear some soft singing in Giant on the wind. He calms himself so that he can gather a better direction of the song. He breaks off from the group and goes towards the song. Paul takes Ronpip and Lorellai back to the manor.

Vimack continues to wander through town to find the source of the song alongside with Dorizoul.

A halfling enters the private with Evan. A cart of papers follow him in. The halfling has a distinct level of concern in his voice but Evan takes no immediate notice. The halfling tells Evan that the Noble House of Urie has a substantial bank of gold under their name, but also substantial debt that is now under the responsibility of the Noble House of Escence. Evan signs off on the exchange and heads back home.

Vimack has been tracking the song that is currently taunting him. He reaches the north eastern part of town, coming upon a bridge where Dorizoul is standing there, oddly alone without her followers and unarmed. Vimack calls out to her, and though she does not give apparent heed, she hears him. Vimack sees that she is staring into the water below. Vimack inquires what she is doing here, and she explains that this was part of The Pilgrimage of Giants that the Old Ones took. She informs him that the district is called Pilgrim's End, and the bridge Lover's Crossing, related to the tale of two Frost Giant Lovers from opposing mountains who could not be together. She is cryptic during the entire exchange but Vimack is catching on. She makes an odd request for Vimack to deliver a stone ring to a hole stone hiding within the canal below the bridge. Vimack takes the ring and dives into the water. The current is strong and is beginning to pull Vimack away from the bridge. Though the water obscures his vision, he sees a standing stone with a hole, and swims towards it. He drops off the ring within the hole. It disintegrates from his hand, the hole stone and glows with Goliath runes, striking his hand with light, and deep dark blue tattoos come from his hand and finger. Suddenly, he feels where North is at all time and has a sensation of the lover's embrace. He returns to the bridge, coughing water out but returns nonetheless. Dorizoul is no longer at the bridge, which saddens Vimack. Though he notices a piece of wood with giant's runes carved into it reading, "Hope to see you again..." With nothing more needed, he heads back to the manor.

The party regathers at the manor and tries to redetermine what they want to do. Evan would like to Identify the magic items they acquired while the party rests at the Ecsence manor. Evan starts with the gauntlets, and find they are the Evan starts With the gauntlets, and find they are the Gauntlets of Heavenly Ascension. He finds the wand of fireballs is in fact the Wand of Fireballs. Boots of Speed. The Steel Gauntlets are the Gauntlets of Flaming Fury.

Following the next day, a messenger from the Noble House of Foo arrives at the Escence manor, requesting him and the party to visit the estate of the Noble House of Foo at their earliest behest. Vimack has been stewing about the noble who interfered with the battle, and finally connects the dots between the noble and the colors of the house they were associated with. Together, they plan for the next day of Session 12...
