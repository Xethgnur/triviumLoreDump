Session 14

Following Session 13:

Evan Escence, Vimack Badger, Lorellai, and Ronpip Thimble-bimble seek to infiltrate the House Foo estate at night in an attempt to deliver information to Duan Ping. Ronpip has been sky dropped by Evan from above, in a failed attempt to Feather Fall an Invisible Gnome.

Ronpip creates an illusion of a cloaked figure to capture the inside patrol's attention. He moves the Silent Image towards the wall as though he is fleeing. Evan shifts his coloration to be darker so that he is less apparent while in flight. He watches overhead, and sees that there are several patrols both inside the walls outside of the house, and outside the walls of the estate. The patrols within the walls have been alarmed and are chasing after the Silent Image. They try to capture it in a triangulated attack as it dives into the bush, but find that their staffs pass through it, and appear to be confused. Vimack and Lorellai notice that the nearest outside patrol is alerted to something, and Vimack feels an overwhelming sense of disappointment. Vimack begins to approach the patrol, though his disappointment distracts him from being stealthy, while Dorizoul keeps up with him quietly. Lorellai keeps up as well, just as loud. The patrol sees them, and move towards the gate and face down Vimack and Lorellai who seem to be approaching quite aggressively.

Meanwhile, inside, Ronpip sneaks between bushes while the inward patrols are distracted by his Silent Image. Evan is still trying to search for Ronpip, and channels energy into his eyes to give him dark vision. He lands on the roof, knocking off a tile and quickly casts Feather Fall on the roof tile before it can expose his position, and potentially put Ronpip in danger.

Vimack maintains the distraction of the outside patrols, using his new tattoo to channel arcane energy as he rages, and frost begins to build at one of the patrolmen's legs. Lorellai aims her shield at the same patrolman, and a beam of dark green energy, and he is visibly weakened from the attack. The patrol outside surrounds Vimack and collectively try to grapple and apprehend Vimack, imprisoning him in a prison of boa staffs, restraining him.

Evan has taken notice to this, and attempts to distract the patrols inside the estate walls. He summons two shadows that take the form of My Immortal. The patrols are now on high alert as they are suddenly being attacked by monsters. They go in to quickly engage the monsters and destroy one of them, and now they seemed concerned someone is summoning things into their estate. A patrol then spots Evan upon the roof, and throw ninja stars at him.

Vimack calls upon his Animated Shield, which pushes a staff just out of the way, weakening the interlocking hold and giving Vimack just enough wiggle room to throw the staves off of him. Lorellai draws upon her watery origin and summons an ice knife, and tosses it into the fight. It explodes in icey shards, and Vimack has to cast Absorb Elements as a reaction, while the patrol dodges out of the way. Vimack tries to shove the nearest patrolmen but he manages to get out of the way. He tries to rush through them but they hold him back with Attacks of Opportunity and prevent him from getting passed him. More patrols outside are sprinting with incredible speed to meet up with Vimack and Lorellai. They come in and grapple Vimack, but are unable to restrain him just yet.

The remaining shadow moves out and attacks at one patrolman, who is caught off guard. It swipes at him, damaging him and draining some of his strength from him. Evan begins to channel psionic energy and spawns a 60ft radius sphere of "magical" darkness upon the ground, protecting Ronpip from being spotted further. Ronpip has seen everything that is going on, and decides to wait things out for a moment. The patrols throw more throwing stars at Evan, three hitting him in the shadows, and then exit the sphere of darkness momentarily. Evan, pained by the stars, loses his concentration on the darkness, and the shadows dissipate.

Outside, Vimack breaks the grapple. Lorellai goes in for the attack, performing the 1-2-3 combo including her shield to attack, but the patrolman manages to divert her flail attacks out of the way with their staff, but when the shield comes in, he blow knocks him back and he has to recover from the sudden shove backwards. One guy is knocked down into another and they are knocked prone. One of the patrols tries to lock down Dorizoul but she easily hopes over his sweep. The two Lorellai knocked down spin and rise to their feet and go after Lorellai, and she misses with her Opportunity Attack, and they attempt to grapple her, but Lorellai keeps her swings wide to keep them from getting into a lock down condition.

Inside, Evan is being accosted by the patrol that has climbed to the roof to meet Evan. Evan channels the sphere of darkness again, this time centered on himself. Meanwhile, on the ground, Ronpip clicks his boots to increase his movement, and he sneaks below under a bush rather quickly. Evan has attracted a majority of the patrols to the roof, and they all attempt to lob throwing stars into the darkness to where they believe Evan was. Evan can hear the throwing stars whiz passed his head and body, and he reactively casts Shield, blocking the one who manages to hit him. From what little he can see, he can see the patrol watch as a single throwing star bounces back and rolls off the roof.

Lorellai goes in for the attack, swinging her flail and catching a patrolman off guard with the flail's chain. Dorizoul disengages and Vimack casts Spike Growth. The patrol go back in to grapple Lorellai, and they manage to catch Lorellai between swings and lock her down. The ones facing Vimack take a standing leap, impressing Vimack as they leap roughly 8ft into the air straight over him. They go in for the attack.

Ronpip continues to sneak towards the estate in a bush. He opens the sliding door. Evan Misty Steps off the roof, and the patrol in the darkness dive into its center to try and apprehend Evan from last they saw him.

Dorizoul rushes in for a Trample and she tramples some patrolmen who do not expect her to rush in. Lorellai does not break the grapple but attacks while grappled, beating upon the patrolmen who have her pinned. Vimack, not wanting the patrol to be killed, dead man lifts the unconscious patrolman out of the Spike Growth and begins to stabilize him to make sure he does not bleed out. The remaining patrolmen try to trip Vimack, but he lowers his body and flexes, taking the sweeping attacks like a champ and stopping them. They try to trip Lorellai, and although she stumbles a bit, she catches herself and stands strong.

Evan takes to the air from the ground and gets over the wall as quickly as possible. He witnesses Vimack and Lorellai standing their ground against the monk patrols taking them on. Ronpip makes it into the sliding door and enters the estate. He quickly does his best to work through the estate to find Duan Ping. He knows that the nobles are likely to sleep upstairs, so heads to the second floor. Upon the hallway, he comes upon several doors with various animals carved into individual doors. He chooses to make his way to the room with the Tiger carved into it.

Critical Damage!

Vimack takes a critical hit from a monk patrolman, striking in unison after Vimack dodges their kicks.

Ronpip quietly opens the door to peak inside to see who is inside. A flash and fast unsheathing of a sword strikes at Ronpip. But just barely misses Ronpip, inches from striking him across the face, its wielder Duan Ping. Ronpip can see the controlled malice on his face from being a trained warrior of the long sword, and he suddenly switches his expression as he realizes it is Ronpip at his doorway. Confused, he starts demanding Ronpip to explain why he is in the hallway, and Ronpip quickly tries to calm him down and to be quiet. He hands him the scroll they took from Xun Fen. Ronpip steps into the room so he is no longer in the hallway. Duan Ping reads the scroll, and expresses disgust. Ronpip also reveals that Qui Chun has a secret basement that stored Shadow Snapper products, as well as the Royal Healers noting the former Emperor was potentially poisoned. Duan Ping is heavily filled with disgust. He pulls a chime out of his robes and taps his scabbard. The patrol outside stops fighting suddenly.

Duan Ping is pleased the information as brought to him, and apologizes for the defensive welcoming. Ronpip shares his condolences, and says he should probably leave. Duan Ping tells him as last words to let his master (Evan) to meet him at the Pillars of Archons for an important message he needs to express to everyone. Ronpip acknowledges, and Dimension Doors outside the estate from the second floor, and lands upon the ground like the hero he is. Ronpip looks up and looks around for his friends or anyone, but is disappointed no one saw this feat.

Vimack and Lorellai help the monks back to their feet and help them. Vimack kindly thanks them for the bout, despite them not knowing who they were or why they were there. They bow in respect but say no words. Vimack bows back.

Evan reappears to Vimack and Lorellai. Vimack voices his odd feeling of disappointment; Evan confesses he misses the Feather Fall but Ronpip admits he was just surprised but otherwise is unbothered. Ronpip relays he gave the information to Duan Ping and wants to see him at the Pillars of Archons. Evan confesses he is tired and has throwing stars in his chest. Vimack pulls one out and keeps it. Ronpip takes the other one. The party returns to the estate, and Paul greets the party, and starts a bath for the party per request. The party rests, and in the morning, they go to meet Duan Ping at the Pillars of Archons in Session 15.
